How Sorting is Ordered

Relevance is determined by two factors (1) the number of times your search terms appear in a document and (2) the proximity of those search terms to each other.

Weighted is based upon the location of the keywords in a document (hits). Hits found in one document field will be assigned priority (weighting) over hits in another document field.
